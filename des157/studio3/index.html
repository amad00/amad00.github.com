RULES

  • There are two players.
  • The player whose turn it is rolls the dice.
  • The total of the roll is added to the current player's score.
  • If you roll a one, your turn is passes over to the next player.
  • After each roll, you call roll again, (assuming a “one” was not rolled) or pass your turn.

How to Win

The first player to get 30 points or higher wins.

Be Careful!

If a player rolls two “ones” (snake eyes), that player's current score gets zeroed out, and they have to start over, accumulating points from zero.
