About Me

I graduated from UC Davis with a bachelor’s degree in Computer Science and Design. I am currently based in the Bay Area.

I am interested in Front-End Development and UI/UX, as well as Design (Product, Web, Graphic). I was first introduced to design and coding in my high school which sparked my passion for those subjects and eventually led me to pursue a design and computer science major in college.

During my time in college, I took courses in graphic design, UI/UX, human centered-design, as well as web development. In taking these courses, I found that I really enjoyed the design process from research and brainstorming to wire framing and working on responsive and inclusive designs. I also enjoy taking my designs and building them into websites.

I’ve worked with a number of different programs and languages, some of which I utilize more than others on a day to day basis. However, I am always open and willing to learn new programs for different projects and opportunities.

I’m looking to pursue a career in Design or Front-End Development and to continue to expand my knowledge and technical skills as a designer, as well as to continue to learn the process of designing inclusive and accessible products.

When I am not designing, I am either reading, or out walking my dog.
