Is home where we are born or where we live? Is home a right or is it earned? Is home a place or is it the people we live with or is it a certain relationship to nature?

We live on stolen land, the home of many Indigenous communities. During this tumultuous year in which we have seen social and racial inequality laid bare, many of our relationships to home have been questioned and rethought.

As we continue to question what home is we are seeking your creative responses.

We are inviting contributions that we will share over the coming months. The prompt is simple: Fill in the blank, provide a longer response or upload an image to answer the question “Home is ____”.

Thank you for participating!
