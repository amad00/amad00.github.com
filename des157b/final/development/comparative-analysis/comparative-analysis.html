Comparative Analysis

Unpigeon

I thought this project was very beautiful. I liked the minimalist and simple aspect of it. It felt very calming. I was initially a little confused about what would result from taking this quiz but I thought it was very cool because of how interact it was and visual feedback you got as you rated each word. I do like the minimalistic aspect of it, however I felt like I needed more because I found myself playing around with the slider and giving each word a random rating. However, I liked the way it was designed and how calming the site was.

Beyond Beauty

I really liked the message of this website. I think the beauty and simplistic but impact nature of this website helped focus my idea and helped to develop it a bit more. I really liked the integration of quotes. I thought the song and animation really made this site more effective. I thought the mouse visuals helped to explain interactive parts of this site. However what was a little confusing to me, was once you clicked on one of the four celebrities, it seemed going back to choose another one was smoothly integrated into this site. I thought the audio of the celebrities speaking with the text on the screen was very powerful. I thought the simple animation were effective, such as the the moving circle. I really liked that and would like to include something similar on mine.
